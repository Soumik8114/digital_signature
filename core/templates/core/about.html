Key Features

  • Automatic Key Generation: Secure RSA-2048 key pairs are automatically created for every new user.
  • Encrypted Private Keys: Your secret private key is always encrypted in our database, ensuring only you can use it.
  • User-Owned Files: You have full control over your documents, with the ability to manage, sign, and delete them.
  • Portable Signatures: Download a simple JSON signature file that contains everything needed for verification.
  • Public Verification: An easy-to-use tool for anyone to verify a signature without needing an account.

Benefits

Authenticity: Cryptographically prove who signed the document.

Integrity: Guarantee that the document has not been changed or tampered with since it was signed.

Non-Repudiation: The signer cannot deny having signed the document, providing strong legal evidence.

Efficiency: Reduce paperwork, save time, and streamline your workflows in a secure, digital format.
